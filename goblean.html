Rules

Goblean are small creatures which live all around us. There are hidden and stay immobile while you are watching them. But they have one default! They all have a tatoo which looks like a bar code (which is called EAN13).


Enter the EAN code of the creature you have catched in this application and it will reveal its original form.

Enter the EAN code of a second creature (catched by a friend) and let them fight

At each round you can select an attack which will be more or less powerful depending of your goblean stats and the defense of your opponent.

If your goblean survive, it will be decalred champion of the Goble-aena. (Then it can be defied by another challenger)
